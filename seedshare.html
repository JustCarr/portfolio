Project Overview
Seed Share is an online platform that connects members of the community with a way to source trusted locally grown organic produce and provide growers the ability to sell or trade their excess produce.
The Problem
Health & eco focused users struggle to source organic fresh produce that is fresh, local and pesticide free. Sustainable choices that benefit the users health, the environment and community are hard to find.
Project Objectives
// Understand the process people go through in acquiring fresh produce?

// Discover how people feel about sourcing produce from varied places?

// Assess the tools and logistics people use to share their produce.
USER PERSONA
Cody, Expat living in Manila

Cody 31, is an Australian expat living with his young family in Manila, they have a good life, but are saddened by the surrounding poverty. Cody wants to make a difference in his community, but he doesn’t know which charities are trustworthy. He’s not sure where to invest his money for maximum impact and help people in a meaningful way.
